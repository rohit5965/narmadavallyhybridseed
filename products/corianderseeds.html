Cultivation Guidelines

Planting Instructions

  • • Sowing time: November–December
  • • Spacing: 20 cm x 5 cm
  • • Seed depth: 4–5 cm
  • • Seed rate: 100–120 kg/hectare

Water & Nutrient Management

  • • Requires 400–450mm rainfall
  • • Irrigate at crown root, tillering, and grain filling stages
  • • NPK: 120:60:40 kg/hectare
  • • Add FYM: 5 tonnes/hectare before sowing
