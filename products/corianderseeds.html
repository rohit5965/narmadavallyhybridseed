Cultivation Guidelines

Planting Instructions

  • • Sowing time: February–March & September–October
  • • Spacing: 20 cm x 5 cm
  • • Seed depth: 1–2 cm
  • • Seed rate: 8–10 kg/hectare

Water & Nutrient Management

  • • Requires 300–350mm rainfall
  • • Irrigate at flowering and seed development stages
  • • NPK: 40:30:20 kg/hectare
  • • FYM: 5 tonnes/hectare before sowing
