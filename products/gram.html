Cultivation Guidelines

Chickpea is a major pulse crop in India, and our varieties are tailored for both early and timely sowing with high resistance and excellent returns. Perfect for sustainable and commercial farming alike.

Planting Instructions

  • • Sowing time: October–November
  • • Spacing: 30 cm x 10 cm
  • • Seed depth: 5–6 cm
  • • Seed rate: 75–100 kg/hectare

Water & Nutrient Management

  • • 1–2 irrigations during flowering and podding
  • • NPK: 20:60:40 kg/hectare
  • • Apply Rhizobium & PSB inoculation to seeds
  • • FYM: 5–8 tonnes/hectare before sowing
