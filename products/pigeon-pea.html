Cultivation Guidelines

Planting Instructions

  • • Sow during Kharif season (June-July)
  • • Spacing: 30cm x 15cm
  • • Seed depth: 3–4 cm
  • • Seed rate: 15–20 kg/hectare

Water & Nutrient Management

  • • Rainfall: 600–650mm required
  • • Irrigation: 2–3 if needed
  • • Fertilizer: NPK 25:50:25 kg/ha
  • • FYM: 5–10 tonnes/ha
